About

BARcamp Chicago is Chicago's longest running unconference. It is a conference focusing on technology and entrepreneurial topics that is free and open to the public. All talks are given by the attendees of the conference. The schedule and talks are determined the day of the event. We have 3 tracks for talks: development, entrepreneurial, and miscellaneous.

Venue

Pumping Station One (3519 N. Elston Ave) will be hosting BARcamp Chicago. The doors will open at 9am on Saturday Sept 22nd with an introduction starting at 10am and talks following. The space will be open all night for any attendees that want to camp out or stay the night at the event. Talks will start back up on Sunday September 23rd at 10am and the the talks will end at 5pm on Sunday.
